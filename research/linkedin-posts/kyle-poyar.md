25 tactics to start & grow a B2B newsletter — from writing Growth Unhinged & growing it to 80k+ subscribers ⤵️

THE BASICS

1. Pick a name that’s short & memorable. 
2. Check that the domain is available (don’t skip this!). 
3. Choose a “from” name for your emails. I recommend a person’s name. 
4. Create a min. viable visual identity. 
5. Write a one-sentence logline for your newsletter. This is your promise to readers. 

WRITING YOUR NEWSLETTER 

6. Find stories that people care about. I like to use a content <> community flywheel. 
7. Niche down, even more than you think. 
8. Maintain a consistent publishing cadence.
9. Sending at the same time (probably) doesn’t matter. (Although CJ disagrees.)
10. Tactical and specific beats high-level. Give the reader something they can apply immediately. 
11. Use AI as a brainstorming partner. 
12. Ask AI to become your research assistant. I <3 Deep Research mode. 

GROWTH LOOPS

13. Set the tone with your welcome email. 
14. Promote your posts on other platforms like LinkedIn. 38% of new subscribers say they first heard about Growth Unhinged from LinkedIn, 6x more than ChatGPT. 
15. Create a LinkedIn company page for your newsletter. This is free & easy. 
16. Feature your newsletter in your social media profiles.
17. Turn on recommendations and reach out to others in your niche.
18. Do the SEO basics. Set up Google Search Console, edit SEO titles & descriptions. 
19. Pitch guest posts to other newsletters. 
20. Set up a simple referral program. 

ADVANCED PLAYS

21. Start an onboarding survey for new readers. I ask 3 questions. 
22. Build your first automated email flows.
23. Optimize your deliverability rates. Mine went from 90.3% to 96.3%. 
24. Quantify your subscriber LTV. 
25. Test paid Boosts.

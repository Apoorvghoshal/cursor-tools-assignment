We just shipped a big batch of updates at Loops:

1. Markdown in the editor. 

Type bold/italic/code and lists directly. We also auto-convert Markdown on paste; use Cmd/Ctrl+Shift+V to paste plain when you need it.


2. Scale & trust. 

We crossed 1,000,000,000 emails sent and completed SOC 2. Our trust portal and DPA are live to speed up security reviews.


3. Power features.

• Idempotency keys so retries don’t double-send.
• CC/BCC (Settings → Tracking) to create simple alert flows or silent copies.
• Cmd/Ctrl+D to duplicate blocks.
• Vertical align in columns for cleaner layouts.


4. Email rendering improvements. 

Better Superhuman dark mode support, stricter MJML handling, and smarter mobile column stacking so designs hold up across clients.
